What is Foodio doing?

-->Foodio matches you with people with similar tastes according to your food taste with the information you enter.

How can you use Foodio?

1- You should download it from App Store or Google Play Store.

2- Enter your informations and click on the register button.

3- After registration you have to answer a quiz.

(The quiz includes a few questions about your food taste.)

4- Edit your profile settings for better matching.

5- That's all. You are ready for matching&chatting.
